Morpheus Dvorak layout

The layout focuses on not changing Symbol locations when on the same hand. This cuts down on confusion when switching between Qwerty and Dvorak. For example []=/ are in the same locations. Underscore and dash and kept in the original Dvorak locations because of their frequency in computer programming and because they switch fingers unlike the []=/ in Qwerty to Dvorak.
